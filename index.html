About Me

I'm Julie, and I wear many hats

except not so much actual, physical hats

I have professional experience in video editing, audio production, graphic design, electronics repair, low-voltage electical installation (ethernet+A/V), and technical support

On my own time I've built custom PCs, voided thousands of warranties, installed Linux onto everything I could get my hands on, built and managed my own servers at home and in the cloud, dabbled in programming, and put together this website you're reading

I'm a lifelong musician and have been gigging since I was a teenager. I've done the majority of the graphic design for my main band Ghost Town Radio and over the years have played with too many acts to count

This blog is primarily for tech-related projects. For my design, multimedia, and musical oeuvre, see juliehill.rocks
